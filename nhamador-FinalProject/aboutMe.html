ABOUT ME

I am currently studying at Arizona State University for my Undergraduate Degree. I was born in Germany and moved around a lot as a kid and lived in Oklahoma, Hawaii, and finally Arizona. Some of my favorite painters are Elaine and Willem de Kooning, Francis Bacon, Walter Battiss, and Graham Sutherland. I am currently exploring abstraction and how far I can distort a symbol before we have trouble grasping it. Some of my favorite painitngs look like an almost complete mess at first, but the longer you focus on the piece the more it comes into focus.
